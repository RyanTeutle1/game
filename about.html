Game Evaluation

When I think of a game's definition I like to think that a game is something which can be played with others or by yourself, whether it be for fun or for a challenging experience. Games are meant to be entertaining and they serve that purpose to bring you joy and happiness while you play them.

My Gamer Types

When I took the Bartle test I answered some questions and in the end my results came out, 'ASEK' which stands for Achiever, Socialiser, Explorer, and Killer. I tested a higher percentage for Achiever which connects players to their games that they play, me specifically I enjoy striving for the better things in a game, whether it be collecting the most equipment or completeing something to completion.
